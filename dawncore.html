DAWNCORE

A speculative genre of music, made in collaboration with Alice dos Reis
Lecture presented at http://www.hoffnung3000.de in Berlin.

Based on the genre ‘Nightcore’ Alice and I imagined a new sub-genre. The music was designed to be easy to replicate. Its creation follows a simple and set algorithmic process.

Installation by Alice dos Reis at Anexo, Lisbon. Curated by Marta Espiridão and Ana Cristina Cachola
Nightcare, 2018
