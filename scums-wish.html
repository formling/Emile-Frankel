SCUM’S WISH

An interactive video-game piece written for one player + different configurations of performers.

Multiple sci-fi videos can be found within the game world, each depicting a future scenario viewed through the borders of differing windows: luxurious ocean liner; desert penthouse; wasteland glasshouse; fantasy jet.

Premiered by Ensemble Maze @ Gaudeamus Muziekweek

Performed again by Rubik’s Collective @ Kenneth Myer Auditorium
