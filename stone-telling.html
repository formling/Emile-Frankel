STONE TELLING

An interactive videogame installation made in collaboration with Luke Deane. 5 Players, physical controllers.

“First Promise of the Stone Streamer: Let us always meet each other with a smile, for the stone is the beginning of love. (6503 B.C.)”

Presented at Korzo Theatre, The Hague. 10th - 12th of May at festival Voorjaarsontwaken.
Controls featured soil, stainglass, and a magnifying lens pointed towards a mouse laser.
Made in Unity
Music and Game Design Shared
